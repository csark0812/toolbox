# Tone (distilled)

Edit this file with your voice rules. Keep Slack dumps and raw samples in `personal/raw/` only.

## Style

- Prefer plain language over jargon when both work.
- Short paragraphs; one idea per beat.
- No hollow openers ("Great question!", "I'd be happy to...").

## Structure

- Lead with the answer or decision, then context.
- Bullets for scanability; prose for nuance.

## Avoid

- Corporate filler, sycophancy, emoji decoration.
- Over-bold and over-backtick decoration.
